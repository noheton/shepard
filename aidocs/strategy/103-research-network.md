### §1.1 What this doc is **not**

- Not a personnel directory — only public scholarship + institutional affiliations are mapped.
- Not a citation count beauty contest — bridge-edges (cross-cluster co-authorships) outweigh raw output.
- Not exhaustive — query budget capped per `feedback_agent_dispatch_discretion.md`; honest gaps surfaced in §2.5.

### §1.2 Discipline (from the original brief + memory)

- **Honest-reconstruction.** Every person who returns 0 hits across queried databases gets that noted explicitly.
- **Privacy.** Only public publications + institutional affiliations. No personal email, no home institution if private, no inference of demographics.
- **Citation hygiene.** Every multi-DB finding that becomes a thesis-relevant reference gets a bib entry in `docs/_data/references.bib`. Not every OpenAlex hit is bib-worthy.
- **Graph cap.** Mermaid network (§4.5) is capped at 15–20 nodes; edges are the value.
